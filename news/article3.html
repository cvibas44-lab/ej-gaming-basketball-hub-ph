MLBB teams tighten their late-game discipline

July 15 • By EJ Gaming Team

Teams are finding that less chaos and more structure often produce better results in the final minutes of a match. Clear assignments, better wave states, and stronger rotation habits are leading to more consistent victories.

That shift is helping teams adapt faster to the most volatile phases of the game.
