Why the current NBA season feels different

July 16 • By EJ Gaming Team

Fans are noticing a faster pace, more versatile lineups, and a greater reliance on bench scoring. Those changes are making games feel more dynamic and less predictable.

The modern schedule also emphasizes rest and matchups over volume, leading to stronger tactical decisions and more dramatic swings in momentum.
