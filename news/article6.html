How community tournaments shape local talent

July 12 • By EJ Gaming Team

Community tournaments matter because they create the first stage of pressure for players who are still developing confidence. They teach discipline, teamwork, and how to perform under a spotlight.

Many of the most recognizable names today started in smaller events that printed the lessons needed for larger stages.
