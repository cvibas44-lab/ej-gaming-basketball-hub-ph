Wellness

The art of building a winning game-day routine

July 11 • By EJ Gaming Team

Consistency matters more than intensity when preparing for a big game. A simple routine can help athletes and gamers reduce stress while sharpening focus.

The best habits are often quiet ones: enough sleep, steady nutrition, and a few moments of deliberate calm before the match begins.
